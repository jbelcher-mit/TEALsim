Magnetostatic Pendulum Same Pole:

The Magnetostatic Pendulum Same Augmented is a simulation consisting of one magnetic dipole that is free to move swinging on a pendulum of radius R, and one stationary magnet dipole fixed on a circular arc with a radius of R + L at an angle to the vertical. The dipoles are both vertical but opposite in orientation. You can change the magnetic moment of the swinging dipole compared to the stationary magnet, but only do that when the pendulum is just at the top of its arc, otherwise energy will not be conserved through the swing.

Use the Start button to start simulation. Use Pause button to pause simulation. Use the slider in the panel labeled "Ratio |m/M|" to change the dipole moment of the swinging dipole.
