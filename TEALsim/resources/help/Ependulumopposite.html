The Electrostatic Pendulum Opposite Charge:

The Electrostatic Pendulum Opposite Charge is a simulation consisting of one electric charge that is free to swing on a pendulum and one stationary charge on the plate below the pendulum. The two charges have opposite signs. You can change the charge of the swinging charge compared to the stationary charge, but only do that when the pendulum is just at the top of its arc, otherwise energy will not be conserved through the swing.

Use the Start button to start simulation. Use Pause button to pause simulation. Use the slider in the panel labeled "Ratio |q/Q|" to change the charge of the swinging charge.
